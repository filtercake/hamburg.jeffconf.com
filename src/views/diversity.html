Diversity @ JeffConf Hamburg

JeffConf is a community organised event focusing on Serverless technologies and the changing face of application development. We aim to make it an inclusive conference welcoming people from diverse backgrounds. To reach our goal, we are going to allow people who are part of an underrepresented group in tech to obtain a free Diversity Ticket. We will keep you posted!
